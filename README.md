<h2 style="border-bottom: none; padding-bottom: 0; margin-bottom: 0;">
  🧑‍💻 About Me:
</h2>

<p>
  I am <strong>Stephen Baraka</strong>, a <strong>Software Engineering</strong> student from <strong>Kenya</strong> with a strong interest in technology, finance, and problem-solving. My journey in technology began with a curiosity about how software systems work and how they can be used to solve real-world challenges.
</p>

<p>
  Through academic studies, personal projects, and continuous self-learning, I have been developing skills in programming, software development, databases, and system design. Currently, I am focused on strengthening my Software Engineering foundation while exploring areas such as <strong>Financial Technology (FinTech)</strong>, <strong>Artificial Intelligence</strong>, and data-driven systems.
</p>

<p>
  Over the next few years, my goal is to specialize in <strong>Financial Software Engineering</strong> — combining software development with financial systems, data analysis, and intelligent decision-making. I am particularly interested in building applications that support financial operations, automate processes, analyze data, manage risk, and create value for businesses and individuals.
</p>

<p>
  I am fascinated by how technology is transforming the financial industry, from digital banking and payment systems to investment platforms and financial analytics. My ambition is to contribute to this transformation by building <strong>reliable, scalable, and intelligent financial software solutions</strong>. I believe that strong engineering, analytical thinking, and continuous learning are the foundations for creating technology that makes a meaningful impact. 🚀
</p>
